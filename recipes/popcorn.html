How to make microwave popcorn.

Put it in the microwave dummy!

  1. Remove plastic from around popcorn bag.
  2. Open microwave door.
  3. Insert popcorn.
  4. Close microwave door.
  5. Set timer on microwave.
  6. Press the start button.

Ingredients

  • Bag of microwave popcorn.
  • microwave.
